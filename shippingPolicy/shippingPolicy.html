Shipping policy

We DO NOT ship our bouquets on all US.

Fleurs De Café offers flower delivery/pick up around Marlboro area. We also provide same-day flower delivery for your last minute gift needs in Marlboro NJ, as well. A local delivery fee of $6 will be added to each order, for each address.

FOR COFFEE BEANS:

We offer FREE SHIPPING on all US orders of $59 or more.

The time frame for order delivery is divided into two parts:

Processing time: Order verification, tailoring, quality check, and packaging. All orders are sent out within 24 hours after the order is placed.

Shipping time: This refers to the time it takes for items to be shipped from our shop to the destination. We use UPS and USPS for our shipments. Both carriers are experiencing COVID-related delays. You can find out more about each carrier's service updates and alerts by following the links below:

UPS Service Alerts: https://www.ups.com/us/en/service-alerts.page

USPS Service Alerts: https://about.usps.com/newsroom/service-alerts/

USPS Updates: https://faq.usps.com/s/article/USPS-Coronavirus-Updates-Expected-Delivery-Changes

Delivery after processing and leaving the shop usually takes about 5 to 7 business days to arrive at their destination but can take longer or reach earlier from time to time.

If you have any additional questions, we will be more than happy to assist you, please feel free to reach out to us at order@fleursdecaf.com for support.
