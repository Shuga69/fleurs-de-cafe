About Fleurs De Café!

Bonjour!

We’re a creative and a beautiful flower + coffee shop located in New Jersey. Our flowers and coffee grow in the most scenic edges. It all begins with the flowers, obviously. Ours come straight from the best eco-friendly farms in Ecuador, Colombia, and the Netherlands so they can make a long-lasting impression. We’re talented and have floral design experience and use seasons as inspiration to design beautifully unique bouquets. Our coffee is chosen and established with great love and greater yet - consideration of origin, flavor profile and quality. All of these components help us to deliver consistent, exceptional coffee with true approachability. We believe fresh flowers and delicious coffee can brighten a room as well as your life and shouldn’t be an expensive indulgence. We offer arrangements for every kind of budget, as well as pre-paid subscriptions that will keep you in blooms all year round. At Fleurs de Cafe, every detail is carefully considered. We embrace the natural quirks and beauty of our blooms. We let the flowers speak for themselves and coffee set our hearts a flutter! We cherish the friendships we make with our clients and are thrilled to be apart of someone's most special day. Feel free to ask us any question, any time. We know the story behind every petal and every bean in our shop.

We’re excited for you to meet our flowers and coffee.

Irena and Julie.
